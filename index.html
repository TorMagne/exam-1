Launchpad information

Launchpad Name

Cape Canaveral Space Force Station Space Launch Complex 40 or CCSFS SLC 40 for short.

Location

Locality: Cape Canaveral. Region: Florida. Timezone: America/New_York. Latitude: 28.5618571. Longitude: -80.577366.

More Details

SpaceX's primary Falcon 9 pad, where all east coast Falcon 9s launched prior to the AMOS-6 anomaly.

Previously used alongside SLC-41 to launch Titan rockets for the US Air Force, the pad was heavily damaged by the AMOS-6 anomaly in September 2016.

It returned to flight with CRS-13 on December 15, 2017, boasting an upgraded throwback-style Transporter-Erector modeled after that at LC-39A.
